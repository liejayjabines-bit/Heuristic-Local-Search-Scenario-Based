1. The Delivery Drone

Question:

3. The Metallurgy Inspiration

Question:
What is this technique called, and what real-world process is it based on?

Answer:
This technique is Simulated Annealing.

10. Balancing the Search

Question:
Explain the difference between exploration and exploitation.

Answer:

Exploration: Trying new paths to find better solutions
Exploitation: Improving known good solutions

Both are important to balance efficiency and effectiveness.
